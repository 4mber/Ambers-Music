bio

Amber is a singer/singwriter from Nashville, TN, who is currently based in Oxford, England. Taking influence from acts such as Led Zeppelin, Brandi Carlile, Patsy Cline, and many others, Amber's music errs a bit on the bluesy, rock & roll side of folk, singer/songwriter.

In 2015 she formed a duo with her husband Andrew Liam Trask called Leigh & Liam. Their debut video was shot in front of the Eiffel Tower and featured a song she wrote for her husband - "Rose Colored Lenses". Check out their "Living Room Sessions", showcasing their takes on many popular songs, shot from their closet. Just kidding, their living room. ;)

When she's not playing music, you will find her cruising the English canal system on her narrowboat with her husband, working on her yoga skillz, or making sweet websites (like this one!).
